The preschool maintains a Sun Smart Policy and we ask you to apply sunscreen available in each room on arrival. Staff will encourage children to keep their hats on at all times when outdoors.

Children are required to be dressed in Sun Smart clothing, which includes clothing that covers and protects as much skin as possible, including the shoulders, back and stomach.

At the beginning of each year all children will be provided with a Griffith Preschool Kindergarten sun safe hat. Children can choose to wear other hats as long as they are sun safe.
